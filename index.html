Nestled in the rolling countryside of Northwest Missouri, Blue Sky Cattery is a small, dedicated breeding program specializing in Oriental Shorthairs with plans to become one of the only Serengeti breeders in the United States. Every kitten is raised underfoot in our home with love, expertise, and 35 years of animal husbandry knowledge.

♥

Raised Underfoot

Our kittens bond with family from day one, surrounded by household sounds, daily routines, and other pets during their critical socialization window. They go home confident, curious, and well-adjusted.

★

35+ Years Experience

From farm management to cow-calf breeding operations, Deanna brings a lifetime of animal husbandry expertise that most cat breeders simply cannot match. The same skills that produce champion livestock produce beautiful, healthy kittens.

✓

Health First

Every breeding cat is health tested. Every kitten goes home fully vaccinated, vet-checked, and with a written health guarantee. We operate a closed cattery to protect our most vulnerable residents.

☘

Country Raised

The peaceful Missouri countryside provides the ideal environment — spacious, quiet, and far from the stress of urban living. Our cats enjoy a real home, not a commercial facility.

Oriental Shorthairs

The Siamese in Designer Genes

Among the most engaging, intelligent, and personality-driven cat breeds in the world. Famously "dog-like" — they greet you at the door, follow you room to room, and hold full conversations about everything happening in the house. Over 300 color and pattern combinations.

Serengeti Cats

All the Wild Beauty. None of the Wild Blood.

One of the rarest domestic cat breeds on earth with only several hundred worldwide. Created by crossing Bengals with Oriental Shorthairs, they look like wild Servals but are 100% domestic — legal everywhere with no permits required.
