About Our Closed Cattery

We operate as a closed cattery to protect our kittens from deadly viruses that can be carried on shoes and clothing. We know this might feel unfamiliar, but it's standard practice among the best breeders worldwide — and it's why our kittens go home healthy.

We offer live video calls so you can see your kitten, our cats, and our home in real time. And you're always welcome to visit in person on pickup day.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How much do your kittens cost?

Oriental Shorthair kittens start at $1,800 for pet quality. Breeding rights are reserved for selected candidates and priced separately. A $500 non-refundable deposit secures your kitten, applied toward the total fee.

Why can't I visit the cattery?

We operate a closed cattery to protect our kittens. Feline panleukopenia has a 90% mortality rate in unvaccinated kittens and can be carried on shoes and clothing. We offer live video calls and welcome you to visit at pickup. This policy is standard among responsible breeders.

When can kittens go home?

Kittens go home between 14-16 weeks of age, after completing their vaccination series and being fully vet-checked. Our current litter (born March 9) will be ready mid-July 2026.

Do you ship kittens?

We prefer local pickup or meeting within a reasonable driving distance. We can discuss delivery arrangements for approved families on a case-by-case basis.

Do Orientals need a companion cat?

Yes. CFA states flatly that "an Oriental Shorthair does not do well as an only cat." They need a companion at their activity level — ideally another Oriental or Siamese. We take this seriously in our application review.

When will Serengeti kittens be available?

Our Serengeti program is in the early development stage. Join our waitlist through the contact form to be the first to know when Serengeti kittens become available.
